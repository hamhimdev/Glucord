Glucord

Glucord, It's a Native Discord.

Glucord is built natively for Windows, to ensure maximum performance and speed, whilst looking beautiful and having minimum hardware usage.

Know this...

...is a side project. A big one.

I have other projects which I have more experience with the tools it uses and I have worked on for longer, so this won't be maintained alot. Doesn't mean I will never check it from time to time, I totally will, I just want you to not expect me to fix anything instantly, coding ain't that easy bud.

...is currently in the works

Many features, including voice calling, forum channels, friend DMs and many more haven't been implemented. This includes stuff like pinning a message or reactions.
I'm working on it though and expect it to be implemented sometime in the timeline of this project! I won't rush anything though.

...won't work as intended

I am very much not the most experienced with C++ and most things I use in the project, so please report ANY issue you find with anything! It's important for me to understand the issue and fix it as soon as possible to improve Glucord as much as I can.

...will have more plans

Possible Linux support and maybe MacOS, I also want to add features that are already possible with Discord modifications, such as message logging and some actually good features, one day though. Thanks for reading this stuff by the way.
